How to Use

  • 1

  • Add the AngularJS ngAnimate module to your project

  • 2

  • Add one of the following files to your app:
    nga.css
    (leaner - recommended animations only)
    or
    nga.all.css
    (more customisable - all animations)

  • 3

  • Add the classes in the 'Classes Applied' section to the element you want to animate

And that is it ... Enjoy!
